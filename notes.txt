problem statement 
	the goal of this project is to scrape the tweets of Persian community on 
	X platform to compare the sentiment analysis and word frequencies in 
	topics and out of topics in three distinct time intervals to gain insights
	of the effects of recent events in Iran had on the way the community 
	posted their tweets and how or up to what extent they have been impacted.
	the data for the project is collected via the most well-known Telegram 
	channels in Farsi spoken community that reflect the most viral tweets 
	from X platform. the said data has gone through cleaning and processing 
	in order to go under analysis and the entire workflow of the project is 
	explained below step by step.

data cleansing 

- Combine all three datasets with their labels
- Extract the main text of the tweet without the channel name and writer username
- Remove duplicates if there's any
- Do deep cleaning, URLs, Hashtags, ...
- Remove empty messages 
- Remove short messages


pre-processing

- Remove punctuation
- Remove (), [], ;, : and etc. because they are used as emojis
- Deal with semi-space before and after words
- Tokenize 
- Check for non-Persian words after tokenization and drop them 
- Stemming
- Remove stopwords, keep negative stopwords for content


EDA

- plot top 10 words per each tl
- hue top 50 words per each tl with POS
- hue top 50 words per each tl with NER
- plot top 10 bigrams per each tl
		
		
sentiment analysis 

- use 3 models to rate each tweet 
- mean the scores for each tweet
- create bins of [-1, -0.1, 0.1, 1] for neg, neut, pos labels
- plot the categories for each timeline
- If tl2 shows decrease in pos, and also increase in pos in tl3, is the difference statistically meaningful?
